## 2. 登记字段

每个 evidence slot 的人工登记至少包含：

- `evidenceId`
- `owner`
- `reviewer`
- `reviewedAt`
- `evidenceRef`
- `disposition`
- `decisionReason`
- `residualRisk`
- `followUpOwner`
- `recoveryCondition`
- `targetReviewDate`

当 `disposition` 是 `accepted-with-risk`、`deferred-with-owner` 或 `blocked` 时，`followUpOwner`、`recoveryCondition` 和 `targetReviewDate` 必须由人工补齐。

## 4. 使用边界

本登记表只能作为人工 evidence intake 的记录入口。允许维护者在真实证据到位后，以独立人工流程填写 `evidenceRef`、`reviewer`、`reviewedAt`、`disposition`、`decisionReason` 和 `residualRisk`。

no automatic approval is granted by this register.

禁止把以下结果自动转为人工批准：

- `convert-check-pass-to-human-evidence`
- `convert-dry-run-pass-to-human-evidence`
- `convert-rc-freeze-to-release-approval`
- `convert-register-exists-to-human-signoff`
- `mark-releaseReady-true`
- `mark-phase29ExitReady-true`
- `mark-phase30EntryReady-true`
- `enable-runtimeExecution`
- `enable-thirdPartyExecution`
- `execute-real-third-party-plugin`
