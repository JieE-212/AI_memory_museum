# 阶段 30 人工复核证据包

更新日期：2026-07-03

Baseline: `2.0.6 / phase30-human-review-evidence-pack`

Phase 30 human review evidence pack: active

Human evidence remains pending.

Phase 30 human review execution ledger: active as `phase30.human-review-execution-ledger.v1`.

The execution ledger is read-only and keeps every evidence slot pending until a human reviewer provides an external evidence reference, reviewer identity, reviewedAt value, decision reason, and residual risk note where required.

## 1. 证据包目标

`2.0.6 / phase30-human-review-evidence-pack` 承接 `2.0.5 / phase30-first-round-planning-closure`，把进入 Phase 30 implementation entry 前必须由人工处理的事项拆成可审阅、可引用、可阻断的证据槽位。

本证据包只定义证据结构和处置规则，不替任何 reviewer 签核，不把 planning、dry-run、Go/No-Go 或 check 结果转换为批准。

只读执行台账 `phase30.human-review-execution-ledger.v1` 会把这些槽位暴露到 `/api/version`、`/api/operations` 和 `/api/operations/export`，用于后续人工复核跟踪；它不提供写接口，不记录签核，不改变任何 guardrail。

本证据包不改变当前工程状态：

- `PHASE=29`
- `APP_VERSION=1.9.48`
- `BUILD_LABEL=phase29-release-exit-final-archive-manifest-preview`
- `releaseReady=false`
- `phase29ExitReady=false`
- `phase30EntryReady=false`
- `runtimeExecution=false`
- `thirdPartyExecution=false`

No human signoff is granted by phase30-human-review-evidence-pack.

## 5. 允许处置状态

人工处置只能使用以下状态：

- `pending`
- `approved-with-evidence`
- `accepted-with-risk`
- `deferred-with-owner`
- `rejected`
- `blocked`

状态含义：

- `approved-with-evidence`：必须有 reviewer、reviewedAt、evidenceRef 和 decisionReason。
- `accepted-with-risk`：必须有 residualRisk、owner 和后续追踪项。
- `deferred-with-owner`：必须有明确 owner、截止条件和仍阻断哪些入口。
- `rejected`：必须说明拒绝原因和需要重做的证据。
- `blocked`：必须说明阻断项、关联 artifact 和恢复条件。

任何 `pending`、`deferred-with-owner`、`rejected` 或 `blocked` 都继续阻断 runtime、release 和 Phase 30 entry。
